Our Story

Built on Brotherhood & Blades

“A great barbershop isn’t just about haircuts. It’s about community, craft, and making every man feel his best.”

BarberKraft was founded by Marcus Johnson in 2011 in Brooklyn. What started as a single chair has grown into a trusted grooming destination.

We believe a barbershop is more than a place for a haircut. It’s a space for confidence, relaxation, and community.
